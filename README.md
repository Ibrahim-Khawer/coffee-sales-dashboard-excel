# Coffee Sales Dashboard


## Business Problem
Coffee companies need to monitor sales performance across different dimensions to optimize product strategy, customer targeting, and regional focus. This project analyzes sales data to uncover insights about customer purchasing patterns and product performance.

## Project Details
**Dataset**: [CoffeeOrdersData](https://github.com/Ibrahim-Khawer/coffee-sales-dashboard-excel/blob/main/coffeeOrdersData.xlsx)
Tools: Microsoft Excel (XLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, PivotTables, Slicers)
Dashboard: [Download Interactive Dashboard](https://github.com/Ibrahim-Khawer/coffee-sales-dashboard-excel/blob/main/coffeeOrdersProject.xlsx)

## Data Preparation
- Data Integration: Used XLOOKUP to retrieve customer details and product information
- Product Mapping: Implemented INDEX-MATCH functions for coffee types and pricing
- Data Transformation: Standardized coffee type names and formatting
- Dashboard Development: Created PivotTables, charts, and interactive slicers

## Analysis Highlights
- Regional Performance: United States dominates sales ($35,638 total)
- Product Analysis: Arabica coffee shows strongest and most consistent sales across all years
- Customer Insights: Top 5 customers represent significant revenue opportunities
- Seasonal Patterns: Higher sales in spring and fall months

## Key Insights
- United States market significantly outperforms other regions
- Arabica is the most popular and consistent coffee type
- Clear seasonal patterns in purchasing behavior
- Top customers contribute substantially to overall revenue

## Business Recommendation
1. Implement targeted loyalty programs for high-value customers
2. Focus marketing efforts on the United States market
3. Leverage strong performance of Arabica in product promotions
4. Use seasonal sales patterns for inventory planning and campaign timing
5. Explore growth opportunities in Ireland and UK markets
