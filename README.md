Business Problem
Coffee companies need to monitor sales performance across different dimensions to optimize product strategy, customer targeting, and regional focus. This project addresses the challenge of tracking and analyzing coffee sales data to enable data-driven decisions for revenue growth and customer satisfaction.

Technical Details
Data Source:

coffeeOrdersData.xlsx - Complete dataset containing order details, customer information, product catalog, and sales transactions

Analysis Workflow:

Data integration using XLOOKUP to retrieve customer details and product information

Product mapping with INDEX-MATCH functions for coffee types and pricing

Data transformation to standardize coffee type names and formatting

Dashboard development with PivotTables, charts, and interactive slicers

Key Findings:

United States market dominates sales performance ($35,638 total)

Arabica coffee shows strongest and most consistent sales across all years

Top 5 customers represent significant revenue opportunities

Seasonal patterns indicate higher sales in spring and fall months

Tools: Microsoft Excel (XLOOKUP, INDEX-MATCH, PivotTables, Slicers)

Recommendation
To maximize revenue growth, the coffee company should:

Implement targeted loyalty programs for high-value customers

Focus marketing efforts on the United States market while exploring growth in Ireland and UK

Leverage strong performance of Arabica in product promotions

Use seasonal sales patterns for inventory planning and campaign timing
